Slim Form Factor

HP has recently released a laptop which may be one of the most impressive laptops on the market. Coming in at a meager 2.45 lbs., the HP Spectre is not the lightest laptop avialable, but it is the thinnest at 0.41" thick. The design of the laptop itseft is also impressive. The laptop's screet is not connected to the back edge like traditional laptops, instead the Spectre's screen is connected to the base by two gold hinges on either side of the laptop's base. The sleek look of the Spectre combined with its black and silver color design makes the Spectre something to behold.
